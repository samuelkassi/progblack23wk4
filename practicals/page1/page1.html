Free online resources:

There are millions of resource available on the internet for free which students use to aid them in their learning. From dedicated sites like Khan Academy to informational tutorial-style videos on video sharing sites like YouTube, free online resources now provide assistance to students who feel that they need extra help, without the extra cost of a traditional tutor.

Remote learning:

The benefits of remote learning were especially obvious during the start of the COVID-19 pandemic where many students on the world relied on online conference software like Zoom to be taught. Remote learning is still important today as it allows for absent students to catch up on their work alot easier. Remote learning has also promoted time-management and self-discipline.

Computing as a teaching aid:

Not only is computing beneficial for students, there are many applications which benefit teachers as well. Software makes it easier for teachers to collect assignments and to know when late assignments are handed in. Furthermore AI technology like Turnitin make it easier for teachers to check for plagarism in students work.
