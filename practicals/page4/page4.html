How has Computer Science helped benefit Communication?

Computer Science has essentially enabled communication greatly by making it faster, more efficient, and more accessible in much more varied forms than before.

Examples of Computer Science Benefiting Communication

Instant World-Wide Communication

Utilizing tools such as gmail, messenging apps or social media apps you are now able to instantly message anyone in the world and even share images with them. This is all made possible with the use of computer science to develop these tools and release them to the public for free or little-to-no cost.

Video Communication

Similarly to instant world-wide communication, computer science enables the ability for app development for applications such as facetime and other video calling services to enable face-to-face instant communication from anywhere in the world.

Networking

Websites such as LinkedIn enable proffesionals to connect, share and communicate to each other about projects, carrer opportunities and other things.
